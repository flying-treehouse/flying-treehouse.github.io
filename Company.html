THE TREEHOMIES

Ellie Alexander

Producer

Ellie Alexander is a sophomore majoring in African and African American Studies with minors in Sociology and Education! Ellie’s favorite role thus far in Flying Treehouse is being the epic Joke Police in Silly Sally, an FTH classic in the 2022 canon. When she’s not being our amazing producer, Ellie is a tutor at Hume, and enjoys baking, board games, and lovely naps! Ellie has two truths and a lie: she has a twin, her feet are half a shoe size apart, and she is a vegan…or is she? Which is the lie? Guess you’ll have to audition and meet her in person! 

Genny Knowles

Publicity Manager

Genny Knowles is a sophomore potentially majoring in Symbolic Systems with a minor in History. She calls Bellingham, WA home, where her dog, Alfonzo, resides! Her favorite sketch thus far in her Flying Treehouse career is probably ‘Cupcake: a Tale of Two Bakeries’ from our fall Throwback Show! Fun facts about Genny: She has 2 1/2 siblings, she has a TJ’s knockoff Taki addiction, she’s technically an Olympian. Outside of Treehouse, she loves climbing with friends, eating cheese, and hanging out with her goofy sweet friends!

Grace Miller

Teaching Coordinator

Grace is a junior majoring in MS&E from Savannah, Georgia. Her favorite sketch she has so far participated in was “Mossed Football,” in which she portrayed an Alabama ball player named Bob who turns Scottish after being bitten by a squirrel of Scottish origins. She has offered us two truths and a lie: 1. Grace attended school in a jailhouse and an abandoned orphanage. 2. She is terrified of tornadoes. 3.She is a dog person. Again, you’ll just have to audition to discover if she indeed is a dog person, scared of tornadoes, and learned counting in a reformed orphanage.
